Marce will reach typhoon category by Nov 5, PAGASA says
Headline | By: Jown Manalo

Severe Tropical Storm Marce (international name: Yinxing) slightly intensified on Tuesday morning, bringing it closer to typhoon status, according to the Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical and Astronomical Services Administration (PAGASA).

“Marce may further strengthen today and reach the typhoon category until it exits our Philippine area of responsibility (PAR),” Pagasa weather specialist Rhea Torres said in mixed Filipino and English during a press briefing.

UP scientists lauded for topping AD Scientist Index 2025
Featured | By: Stephanie Sevillano

The University of the Philippines (UP) lauded its scientists on Monday after they topped other Philippine higher education institutions in the Alper-Doger (AD) Scientific Index 2025.

The AD Scientific Index is a ranking and analysis system of scientific performance that adds value to the scientific output of individual scientists.
